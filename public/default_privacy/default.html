Escapp - Privacy Notice

Last Updated: 21st February 2025

1. What Data We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email, password.
  • Learning Data: Escape room progress, grades.

2. Why We Collect Data

  • Research purposes.
  • Security and platform improvements.

3. Data Retention

Data is stored until the teacher deletes the escape room. Anonymized data is retained indefinitely for research.

In compliance with EU laws, anonymized user data is made available in research data repositories. This data cannot be linked back to individuals.

4. User Rights

  • Users may request data access, correction, or deletion.
  • Requests should be made to the teacher who created the escape room.

5. Data Security

Data is stored securely on EU-based servers.

6. Contact Information

A data protection contact will be available in the future.
